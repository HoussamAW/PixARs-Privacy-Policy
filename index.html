1. Overview

PixARs is a sticker-based app that allows users to create, place, and interact with digital stickers. This Privacy Policy describes what information may be collected through the app and how that information is used.

By using PixARs, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We May Collect

a. Information you provide

Depending on how PixARs works, we may collect information such as:

  • Your nickname, username, or profile name
  • Content you create in the app, including stickers or related metadata
  • Messages or support requests you send to us

b. Information collected automatically

When you use PixARs, certain technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Basic usage data, crash logs, and diagnostics
  • Approximate or precise location data, only if location-based features are used and permission is granted

3. How We Use Information

We may use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve PixARs features
  • Save and display stickers and user-generated content
  • Maintain app security and prevent abuse
  • Fix bugs, improve performance, and analyze app reliability
  • Respond to support requests and user questions

4. Location Data

PixARs may use location data if the app includes features that depend on where the user is located, such as discovering or restoring stickers in specific places.

Location access is only used with your permission. You can manage or revoke location access at any time in your device settings.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary:

  • With service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, database, analytics, or crash reporting tools
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or the public

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

We keep information only for as long as necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

7. Your Choices

You may have the ability to:

  • Stop using PixARs at any time
  • Request help about your stored data
  • Disable permissions such as camera or location in your device settings
  • Contact us to request deletion of your data, where applicable

8. Children’s Privacy

PixARs is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law without parental or guardian consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information without proper consent, please contact us.
