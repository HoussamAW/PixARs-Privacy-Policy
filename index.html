1. Overview

StikAR is an augmented reality sticker app that allows users to place digital stickers in real-world locations and find them again when they return to the same place. This Privacy Policy explains what the app uses to function and how your privacy is protected.

By using StikAR, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Use

a. Username only

To use StikAR, you only provide a username. We do not require your real name, phone number, or other personal identity information.

b. No personal data stored

StikAR does not store personal data such as your full name, address, payment information, or private profile details.

c. Camera and location access

The app requires access to your camera and location because these features are necessary for the core experience. The camera is used to place and view stickers in augmented reality, and location is used to help the app find the stickers again when you return to the same place.

3. How the App Uses Access and Information

StikAR uses only what is necessary to make the app work, including:

  • Allowing you to create and use your username inside the app
  • Using the camera to place and view stickers in augmented reality
  • Using location to help restore stickers when you come back to the same area
  • Maintaining the basic functionality, stability, and security of the app

4. Location Data

Location access is required for StikAR to work as intended. It helps the app associate stickers with places so they can be found again later.

Location is used for app functionality only. You can manage or revoke location access at any time in your device settings, but some app features may no longer function correctly without it.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Because StikAR does not store personal profile data beyond a username for app use, there is no personal account data sold or shared for marketing purposes.

Limited technical information may only be processed when necessary to operate, secure, or improve the app, or when required by law.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited app-related information used by StikAR against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Since StikAR is designed to minimize personal data collection, we only aim to use and keep the minimum information necessary for the app to function.

7. Your Choices

You may choose to:

  • Stop using StikAR at any time
  • Choose a username that does not reveal your identity
  • Disable camera or location permissions in your device settings
  • Contact us with privacy questions or support requests

8. Children’s Privacy

StikAR is not intended to be used in violation of the minimum age rules required by applicable law or platform rules. If you believe inappropriate personal information has been provided through the app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown on this page. Continued use of StikAR after changes means you accept the updated policy.
